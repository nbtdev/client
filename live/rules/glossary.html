Combat Unit
A Combat Unit is any item in an NBT league's associated game that can be played in the game. For instance, in a Mechwarrior-based game, combat units typically are limited to Battlemechs. In a tabletop-based game such as MegaMek or Battletech, the range of combat units playable in the game is much wider and deeper, and can include items such as infantry, tanks, aerospace fighters, and so on.
Industry
Industry is a per-planet quantity that models economic activity in the league, and represents the number of c-bills a planet produces per month. This amount is paid out to the faction on a weekly basis, and is the source of the faction's regular, ongoing income.
Charge Station
A Charge Station in the BattleTech Universe is a celestial device capable of gathering energy from a star system's star(s) and transferring it to the energy source for the Kearny-Fuchida faster-than-light (FTL) drive in a jumpship. In NBT, a charge station allows a jumpship to recharge instantly and continue on its way without having to wait for a full recharge (which typically is 2-4 days long).
Jumpship
A Jumpship in the BattleTech Universe is a stellar vehicle that possesses a Kearny-Fuchida faster-than-light (FTL) drive, and is capable of ferrying other space-faring vehicles along for the ride. In NBT, jumps between star systems are instant, and jumpships can dock zero or more dropships (which in turn contain combat units).
Dropship
A Dropship in the BattleTech Universe is used to carry combat units and commercial goods to and from a planet's surface. Dropships are moved between star systems by docking to a jumpship, and then jumping the jumpship. In NBT, dropships are used to carry combat units, both into battle as well as for peacetime commercial movement of same.
C-Bill
The primary unit of currency in BattleTech Universe. It is typically a low denomination, and this tends to lead to large numbers in NBT, but it allows the bookkeeping to remain simple.
Factory
Factories in BattleTech Universe, and in NBT, produce combat units. A planet may have more than one factory, and a factory may produce more than one type or class of combat unit. Combat units are purchased directly from factory production.
Garrison
A Garrison is an installment of combat-ready forces, typically for defense of a planet or sector. The totality of the combat units present on a planet's surface comprise its garrison.
Allied Garrison
Factions allied with a planet or sector owner may place a garrison of their own on that planet or in that sector. This garrison may assist with a planet's or sector's defense, or optionally handle it entirely on their own.
Cross-Technology
Technology from the opposite faction class (i.e. Clan tech for I.S. factions, I.S. tech for Clan factions).
Forces Declaration
Issued when a battle moves from Pending to Forcedec state, this is a listing of all known conditions for the battle (as played in the game or simulator) as well as all combat units available to each faction for the battle. Opponents are not provided each others' forcedec -- they only see their own.
